Rolls-Royce

Rolls-Royce Motors was a British luxury car manufacturer, created in 1973
during the de-merger of the Rolls-Royce automotive business from the  nationalised
      Rolls-Royce Limited. It produced luxury cars under the Rolls-Royce and Bentley brands.
    Vickers acquired the company in 1980 and sold it to Volkswagen in 1998.
