PORSCHE

Porsche, founded by Ferdinand Porsche in 1931, is a prestigious German automobile manufacturer
           famed for its high-performance sports cars,SUVs, and sedans. The brand's first production car,
            the 356, debuted in 1948, followed by the iconic 911 in 1964, which set standards in sports car
             design and performance. Over the years, Porsche expanded its lineup with models like the Boxster
             , Cayenne, Panamera, and Macan, showcasing its versatility. The launch of the all-electric Taycan 
             in 2019 underscores Porsche's commitment to innovation and sustainability, blending its rich 
             heritage with modern advancements to remain a leader in the luxury and performance automotive market.
